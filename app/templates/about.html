About Site:This site would be useful for both casual fans and hardcore card collectors. Collectors could use this site to find data to buy, sell, and trade their cards as well as build new decks based off of the information provided while casual fans could use the information to sell old, unused cards that collect dust in the corner of their closets. New duelists could also use this site to learn about the TCG.
